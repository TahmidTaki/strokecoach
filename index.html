Back to everyday life with stroke coach

The stroke coach program for people affected by a stroke offers a unique combination: daily training at home or in the practice - always accompanied by your personal coach. In addition, we track your health and progress. Together we will achieve your training goals - even years after the stroke.

I want to participate

The stroke coach training program

With the Neurocoach Digital Lab training program, you always train just right. Our three building blocks ensure this:

Training

With the Neurocoach Digital Lab training program, you train every day and always exactly right. You can get your personal training plan including exercise videos via the Neurocoach Digital Lab app. We adapt the exercises to your goals and progress.

Coaching

Your personal stroke coach accompanies you the whole time. On site in the practice and digitally via smartphone, he will answer your questions about training and all aspects of stroke. With us you never train alone.

Monitoring

We also track your progress and health at home. This will help you reduce the risk of having another stroke. Take control of your health with the Neurocoach Digital Lab training program.

Learn more about the program

And this is how it works

Right at the beginning of the program you will get to know your personal stroke coach. Your stroke coach will explain the course of the program to you and answer your questions.

From this day on, your coach will accompany and support you in your daily training. Celebrate your progress together. And even if you are less motivated, we are there for you!

Find Your Neurocoach Digital Lab

You will find our Neurocoach Digital Lab practices spread all over Germany. In our practices, you can take part in the stroke coach program with an ergotherapy prescription or as a self-payer.

But you can also take part in the Neurocoach Digital Lab program purely online and from home as a self-payer.

Would you like to know where to find your stroke coach? We are happy to help you with your search. Please send us a message.
