Become part of the Neurocoach Digital Lab team

In our interactive training courses we train you to become a Neurocoach Digital Lab. You will learn how to use the Strokecoach training program. The theoretical background and the course of the program (NDL Patient Journey) are conveyed. You will also learn the central importance of coaching and the role of a NDL coach.

As a NDL coach, you will accompany the program participants during the one-year training program. They support and motivate the participants, become the central contact person and make the scientific background of the program understandable. In addition to the coaching, you will be instructed in the use of our innovative rehab management system as well as monitoring and its basics.

Have we piqued your interest?

Simply contact us if you are interested in working with Neurocoach Digital Lab and want to become part of our team.

What awaits you

During the three-day training

Learn the basics and the practical application of the Strokecoach training program,

Find out how the program is tailored to the participants, their individual requirements and goals, as well as how it can be used in everyday life and

what sense and effect the individual components have alone and in combination.

During the training you will receive the Neurocoach Digital Lab Reader, a manual on the theoretical principles and application of the Neurocoach Digital Lab training program. You will also receive your personal access to the digital rehab management system. After the training, all participants will continue to be supported as part of the Coach the Coach program. This is how we ensure the implementation of the theoretical content in your practice.

Next Meetings

The Neurocoach Digital Lab training courses take place in our Germany-wide practices, in cooperating stroke units and rehabilitation centers. We also present the concept at congresses and specialist events.
