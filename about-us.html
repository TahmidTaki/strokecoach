Prof. Dr. Abdul Wahab Abdul Rahman

Advisor of Neurocoach Digital Lab

I started my career with Hewlett Packard and has worked in Singapore and Colorado, USA prior to joining the faculty member of Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 1990. In 2009 I joined the Kulliyyah of Information and Communication Technology (KICT), International Islamic University, Malaysia (IIUM).

My current research are in the areas of understanding and analyzing brain developmental disorder using the EEG and ECG as neuro-cardio signals modeling (NCM) of the brain and the heart.

During my free time, I love to travel and walking.

Norzaliza Md Nor

Head of Neurocoach Digital Lab/ Project Manager

Assalamu’alaikum wmt wbt & Hello,

Zaliza here! I am a lecturer at International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM). I teach Brain Computational Analytics and Probability & Statistics courses under Department of Computer Science, Kulliyyah of Information & Communication Technology, IIUM.

I have been involved with various research study on brain signals by using Electroencephalogram (EEG) Machine since 2010. Analysing EEG data by using various machine learning technique will be my research interest. Being a part of Neurocoach Digital Lab (NDL) team in Malaysia is like a dream comes true, because it offers services that can help society to improve their life style. This is the time, when research can really contribute back to the society. The services will be improved from time to time to ensure that NDL’s client has a very good experience with NDL. Welcome to NDL and your experience with NDL will always be our priority.

During my free time, I love to cooking, travelling and do some window shopping. These are the perfect activities to absorb some energy and make me fresh again.
