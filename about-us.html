Prof. Dr. Abdul Wahab Abdul Rahman

Advisor of Neurocoach Digital Lab

I started my career with Hewlett Packard and has worked in Singapore and Colorado, USA prior to joining the faculty member of Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 1990. In 2009 I joined the Kulliyyah of Information and Communication Technology (KICT), International Islamic University, Malaysia (IIUM).

My current research are in the areas of understanding and analyzing brain developmental disorder using the EEG and ECG as neuro-cardio signals modeling (NCM) of the brain and the heart.

During my free time, I love to travel and walking.

Muna Azuddin

Quality and Multimedia Manager

Assalamu’alaikum wmt wbt,
